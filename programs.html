Our Programs

PERSONAL TRAINING

Looking to improve your game? Take private lessons to up your skills and prepare for league games.

IN HOUSE LEAGUE

Play with LSFC members based around your schedule and ability, and get to know what the club has to offer.

WESTERN PROGRAM

We play with Western University's Squash League so they can get connected to the broader squash community in Canada and worldwide.

CITY LEAGUE

Play with squash players all across London to connect with the community and increase your skills.

JUNIOR LEAGUE

For children and youth who can benefit from playing squash, including those whose parents aren't members.

DEVELOPMENTAL PROGRAM

Want to pursue squash professionally? This is the program to do it, coached by world class squash player and LSFC staff member Jay Francis.
